# nft-transfer-klaytn

Github Page: https://yeonju-kim.github.io/nft-transfer-klaytn/ 

1. Login with your Metamask wallet.
2. Enter the Contract address and click `show NFTs` button to see your NFT images. 
3. You can click image you want to transfer to another account to fill in `Token ID` field. Or you can fill in it manually.
4. Enter the recipient's address in the `To address` field.
6. Click `Send NFT` button and Sign transaction with your metamask wallet.  
7. Done!
